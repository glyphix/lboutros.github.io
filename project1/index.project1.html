Tajawal is a distinctive typeface family designed by Boutros™. It is a low contrast sans serif Arabic (and Latin) typeface family in seven weights. Tajawal follows a modern geometric style while still respecting the calligraphy rules of the Arabic script. Its fluid geometry makes it a distinctive feature ofboth print and web applications, and it also matches beautifully with other Latin typefaces.
